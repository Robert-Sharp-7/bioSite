About Monica

Monica Marie Rocco was born to Joseph and Emily Rocco on March 1st of 1993. She is the second born of two children; her brother Anthony was born 4 years earlier. Monica grew up in Colorado Springs, Colorado where she found a love for hiking and camping. When she wasn't spending all of her time outside, she took piano lessons and enjoyed playing video games. Monica also had a great love for sports. She competed in volleyball, basketball, tennis, and track and field. Eventually finding her real love of sand volleyball.

Monica graduated high school in 2011 and decided she wanted to start a new life, in a new state, and ended up attending Creighton University in Omaha, Nebraska. Monica majored in Mathematics, but in her free time she played intramural volleyball and was the leader of an outreach Girl Scout Troop. She graduated with her bachelor's degree in 2015 and started a job as an underwriter for an insurance company.

After a few months at her new job, Monica decided she wanted to continue her education and enrolled in the MBA program at the University of Nebraska at Omaha. After a semester in the program, Monica decided it was time to change careers and became a data analyst for Berkshire Hathaway. Monica has been working there for about a year-and-a-half now and is planning to graduate with her master's degree in May of 2018.
